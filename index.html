📖 How to Play Black Hole

🎯 Objective

Place tiles so your lowest-value tiles end up closest to the Black Hole. The player whose tiles are worth less near the hole wins.

🔄 Turn Order

  1. Players alternate placing one tile per turn on any empty hex.
  2. Tile values increase each round — both players receive tiles 1, 2, 3 … 22 in the same order.
  3. After all 44 tiles are placed, the single remaining empty hex becomes the ⚫ Black Hole.

🌀 Scoring — The Event Horizon

Hexes are scored in rings of distance from the Black Hole outward:

  • Ring 1 — hexes directly adjacent to the Black Hole.
  • Ring 2 — hexes adjacent to Ring 1, and so on.

Compare each player's tile sum in Ring 1. Lower sum wins immediately. If tied, move to Ring 2. Continue outward until a winner is found.

💡 Strategy Tips

  • 🟢 Save your 1s and 2s — place low tiles near where the Black Hole will form.
  • 🔴 Dump high tiles early (18–22) — push them to the far edges.
  • ⭐ Ring 1 is decisive — winning it ends the game immediately in your favour.
  • 🤔 Player 2 has a slight edge (~52%) — you can react to where P1 plays.
